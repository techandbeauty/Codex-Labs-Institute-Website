How It Works

Admission Process

Our streamlined admission process is designed to identify motivated learners who are ready to commit to their education.

1

Submit Application

Complete our online application form with your background information, program interest, and learning goals. Takes about 10-15 minutes.

2

Application Review

Our admissions team reviews your application within 2-3 business days. We evaluate motivation, commitment, and fit for your chosen program.

3

Admissions Interview

Selected applicants are invited for a 20-minute video interview to discuss your goals and answer any questions about the program.

4

Enrollment

Accepted students receive an enrollment packet with next steps, payment options, and onboarding materials to prepare for class.
